# 060 — Annual Overview ✅ Complete

## Goal

See all 12 months of a year at a glance to spot seasonal patterns and track savings rate.

## Delivered

- `GET /reports/annual?year=YYYY` — income, expenses, net, savings rate per month (always 12 rows)
- Year-level totals + average savings rate across months with income
- Annual Overview page: year selector (← / →), KPI cards, grouped bar chart (income vs expenses), month table
- Savings rate colour-coded: ≥ 20% green, < 0% red, else neutral
- Clamped to current year maximum
